Key Principles  ·  Every meeting has exactly one Accountable (A) owner — the Delivery Manager holds accountability for most delivery meetings, but delegates running to relevant leads.  ·  Stakeholders are kept Informed by default; escalate to Consulted only when scope or priority decisions are required.  ·  Dev & QA Teams are Responsible in execution meetings but should be shielded from governance and reporting meetings to protect focus time.
